Instructions. Please read them carefully.

Welcome to our game! X people, that is you and X-1 others, have joined us.

Your decision.
You will receive 100 coins per round for X rounds. Each round, you will have to choose how many coins to put directly into your personal account, and how many coins to put into a group account.

Return from personal account.
Each coin that you put into your personal account each round goes one-to-one towards your final earnings in the game.

You will play in groups of four.
You will always play in a group of four, and you will be told when the group composition changes. Unless otherwise noted you will therefore play with the same players as in the previous round.

Return from group account.
Each coin that you and the other players in your group put into the group account will be summed up and multiplied by 1.5 to give the group account total. This total will be divided equally between the players in each group.
